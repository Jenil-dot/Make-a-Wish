Make A Wish!

Make A Wish.com operates through its website, browser plug-ins, and apps to serve as a third-party organizer alongside other online gift registry services for weddings, baby showers, birthdays, and other gift-giving occasions.Make A Wish.com also enables potential gift-givers to donate cash via PayPal rather than a physical gift. Although not addressing Make A Wish.com specifically, the Emily Post Institute and others have decried both giving cash as a wedding gift and expanding gift registry beyond weddings to other occasions such as college graduation.Make A Wish has gained notoriety in part due to their interoperability with Pinterest. Shopko joined with www.Make A Wish.com to offer a universal gift registry to its customers. The registry was available in Shopko and Shopko Hometown stores and to online shoppers prior to the company's closure in 2019. Costco has teamed up with Make A Wish.com to offer registry services for engaged couples and expectant parents as well as other universal gift registries.
